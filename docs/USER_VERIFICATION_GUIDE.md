## ðŸ•’ How the Process Works

It takes about 30 seconds and follows these simple steps:

### 1. Camera Access
You'll be asked to allow camera access. This is only used to see "live" landmarks on your face.

### 2. Follow the Prompts
The system will ask you to perform a series of challenges to prove you are a real person:
- **Head Turns**: Turn Left, Turn Right, Look Up, or Look Down.
- **Gestures**: A clear **Nod Yes** (up and down) or **Shake No** (side to side).

### 3. Instant Analysis
Our local AI analyzes the proportions of your face to estimate your age. It checks things like the distance between your eyes and the structure of your jaw.

### 4. Verification Complete
Once you pass the challenges and the system confirms you are an adult, a secure proof is recorded on the blockchain tied to your wallet.
